Discover North Pole Alaska

Santa Clause House

Join us for an exciting tour of old Saint Nick's Workshop.

Reindeer Academy

Come and see the training routine to Santa's famous reindeer.

Iditarod

Come see the start of the Iditarod sled dog races. The Iditarod is an annual sled dog race commemorating the 1925 diphtheria outbreak in Nome, Alaska.

Northern Lights

While visiting North Pole why not take a moment and enjoy the aurora borealis, otherwise known as the northern lights.

Chena Hot Springs

After enduring the cold of Alaska why not soak the chill away in one of Alaska's natural hotsprings.

River Boat Discovery

Sternwheel river boat tours. Ride the Discovery up the Tanana River.

Alaska Oil Pipeline

Trans-Alaska Pipeline System(TAPS) is one of the world's largest pipeline systems stretching from the top of the Alaska at Prudhoe Bay to the bottom at Valdez.

World Ice Art Championships

From February 15th to March 19th come and see the World Art Ice Championships.

Ice Fishing

Join us on the ice as we fish for Rainbow Trout, Arctic Char and Lake Trout.
