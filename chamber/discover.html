Discover North Pole Alaska

Santa Clause House

Join us for an exciting tour of old Saint Nick's Workshop. Here you will see the elves at work as the work round the clock to make toys for all the girls and boys around the world.

Reindeer Academy

Come and see the training routine to Santa's famous reindeer. These Reindeer train year round to provide all the children of the world toys on christmas morning.

Iditarod

The Iditarod sled dog races are an annual sled dog race commemorating the 1925 diphtheria outbreak. 20 brave mushers and their sled dog teams fought to bring life saving medicince to Nome Alaska. The Iditarod race is a grueling 958 mile race from interior Alaska to Nome Alaska.

Northern Lights

While visiting North Pole why not take a moment and enjoy the aurora borealis, otherwise known as the northern lights. These beautiful dancing lights can be seen throughout the winter months displaying vibrant colors of greens, yellows, reds and purples.

Chena Hot Springs

After enduring the cold of Alaska why not soak the chill away in one of Alaska's natural hotsprings. These hot springs are open during the evenings for visitors to soak their cares away under the northern lights.

River Boat Discovery

If the winter months aren't for you North Pole has something to offer everyone. Come take a boat tour on our sternwheel named Discovery up the tanna river. During your river adventure you will be exposed to the rich history of Alaska.

Alaska Oil Pipeline

One of the greatest modern marvels of Alaska is the Trans-Alaska Pipeline System (TAPS) covering 800 miles of Alaskan wilderness with 11 pump stations. TAPS is one of the world's largest pipeline systems stretching from the top of the Alaska at Prudhoe Bay to the bottom at Valdez.

World Ice Art Championships

From February 15th to March 19th come and see the World Art Ice Championships. Ice sculpture artists from around the world congregate to Fairbanks Alaska to compete in a hot blooded battle for the worlds best ice sculpture.

Ice Fishing

One of Alaska greatest tourist attraction is fishing. For any avid angler out there an authentic Alaskan ice fishing trip is a must have. Join us on the ice as we fish for Rainbow Trout, Arctic Char and Lake Trout.
