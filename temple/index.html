Welcome to Temple Inn & Suites helping families come closer together across both sides of the viel. We aim to deliver a comfortable stay by adhering to three principles of the Gospel of Jesus Christ. These Pillars are;

  1. “Thou shalt love the Lord thy God with all thy heart, and with all thy soul, and with all thy mind.” (Matthew 22:37)
  2. “Thou shalt love thy neighbor as thyself.” (Matthew 22:39)
  3. “When ye are in the service of your fellow beings ye are only in the service of your God” (Mosiah 2:17)
