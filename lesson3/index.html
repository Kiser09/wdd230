Site Purpose

Santa Clause delivers presents to an average of +500 million children every year on Christmas eve. Since jolly old Saint Nick started, his endeavor to spread Christmas cheer across the globe has only gotten harder. We elves at the North Pole could no longer keep up with the orders of so many children around the world. The Santas Chamber of Commerce was formed in the North Pole to meet these increasing demands. We aim to provide the youth of the world gifts from Kris Kringle and spark wonder and imagination in their hearts. To fulfill this mission, we elves have three priorities;

  1. Sparking innovation and ingenuity through the private sector. Includes constantly improving methods and technologies available to all citizens of North Pole and improving the city infastructors.
  2. Hosting networking events across the globe to increase partnerships with outside businesses in support of our most crucial time of the year.
  3. Sponsor technical training for various employment opportunities in and out of North Pole
If you're interested in making a childs dreams become a reality, join Santas Chamber of Commerce. Where we make Christmas wishes a reality.
